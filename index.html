About Me

I've always been fascinated by how a few lines of logic can solve massive, real-world problems.

My journey into tech really took off when I started experimenting with HTML and CSS on my father's old laptop. What began as a curiosity soon turned into a career path that led me to a Computer Science degree at IUT.

Today, I'm a backend-focused engineer who loves building systems that don't just work — they last. I spend my time exploring distributed systems and AI engineering, finding ways to make machine learning practical and scalable.

Beyond the code, I've always been drawn to leadership and community. Whether it was leading Notre Dame Science Club or my current role at IUT Computer Society, I believe that life is best enjoyed by people who know how to collaborate and grow together.

When I'm not at my desk, you'll likely find me travelling, trying out new foods, playing video games, or getting lost in a good thriller novel.
